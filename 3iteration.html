Cognify – Third Iteration

Third Iteration was completed in this commit on April 28, 2025

Overview

In the third iteration, we focused on making the app more complete and polished. This phase was more challenging than the previous ones, as it involved deeper Android system features that were new to us and time-consuming to implement. We reintroduced the language option from the first iteration, added two permission toggle buttons that guide users to the settings, and separated the games from the home screen into their own dedicated screens.

Pop-Up Behavior

When the user opened Chrome and remained on it for 15 seconds, a full-screen pop-up appeared, encouraging them to start a cognitive exercise. If the user clicked "Start," they were immediately taken to a randomly selected game within the app.
